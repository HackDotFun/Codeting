$GUN Whitepaper

Tech. Information

Built with Node.js & Telegraf for Telegram integration, @solana/web3.js for in-chat wallet management and Devnet airdrops, Supabase for data storage and REST API, Docker for containerized deployment, and GitHub Actions for CI/CD.

Functionality

$Users invoke /start to generate a Solana keypair in-chat (with Devnet airdrop), invite three others for 4-player Russian Roulette, pay the SPL $GUN entry fee, spin the virtual cylinder, and automatically update scores on Supabase with /leaderboard access.

Rewards

Rewards accumulate as you play and win: a formula based on your $GUN token holdings determines how many game credits you earn, and claims unlock three hours after launch.
